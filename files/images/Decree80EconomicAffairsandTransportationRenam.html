Decree #80 of the Civilian Administration

Economic Affairs and Transportation Renaming

The Chancellor of the Imperial Robloxian Federation, using the powers granted to him by the Constitution of the Federation and the current Laws and Regulations of the Federation, hereby enacts the following:

  1. The Ministry of Economic Affairs and Transportation, on the request of the Economic Affairs Minister, will be renamed to the Ministry of Labour.
  2. All previous regulations on the Ministry of Economic Affairs and Transportation shall apply to the renamed Ministry of Labour.

The following Decree has been granted the force of law, thus making it official.

(Nou_devv)
